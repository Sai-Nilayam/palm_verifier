Objective
---------
This project is verifying Palms of different persons. The idea behind this 
is, the way a Siamese network learns a function to find difference between 
two faces, can we train a Siamese network to find difference between two
Palm images.

Humen can differentiate difference faces. The same way they can also 
differentiate different Palms.
